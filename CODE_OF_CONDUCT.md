# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, focus on technical merit, and keep discussions actionable.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, disclosure of private information, or intentionally disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this standard. Security-sensitive reports should be handled privately through the process in `SECURITY.md`.
